Manned
Guarding

Manned Guarding Security defines the activity of a vetted, professional, and trustworthy security officer, that protects. Manned guarding activity includes any of the following:

  • Guarding one or more individuals against assault or against injuries that might be suffered in consequence of the unlawful conduct of others
  • Site Security Services, Retail security staff, Private Secuirity, Security Receptionist
  • Guarding premises against unauthorized access or occupation, against outbreaks of disorder or against damage;
  • Guarding property against destruction or damage, against being robbed or against being otherwise dishonestly taken or obtained
  • Access control and/or Gate house duties
